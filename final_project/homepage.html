What if?

Hi, I am Junwoo Cheong.


I am a student at Carnegie Mellon University, studying Industrial Design and Human Computer Interaction.


For me, Design is to start with a simple question and end up with an unpredicted result. Knowing what is to come isn't exciting. Great design happens unexpectedly.

Once I asked a wise man what the final product is going to be. and he answered,

"Well, we will see."
